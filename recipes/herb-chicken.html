Herb Chicken


Description

Nothing beats an easy chicken recipe, and I am in love with this simple, creamy herb sauce. My husband’s eyes popped out of his head when he tasted this recipe for the first time. It is so flavorful.


Ingredients

  • chicken(raw) about 2lbs
  • garlic herbs
  • butter
  • bread crumbs
  • Sour Cream

Steps to cook:

  1. Slice the chicken into 2-3 thinner slices.
  2. Sprinkle lightly with salt/pepper and Italian seasoning.
  3. Dredge in flour.
  4. Sear in olive oil and set aside.
